About Hawkins High

Hawkins High is a mysterious school located in the heart of Indiana. Known for its exceptional academics and extracurricular programs, it nurtures young minds while embracing the supernatural and strange happenings around town. Students experience a balance of knowledge, curiosity, and adventure in a world full of secrets.
